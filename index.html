Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to our most commonly asked questions.

What fitness level do I need to join GymFit?

GymFit welcomes members of all fitness levels! Whether you're just starting your fitness journey or are an experienced athlete, our trainers will help you find the right classes and exercises for your current level and goals.

What should I bring to my first visit?

For your first visit, we recommend bringing comfortable workout clothes, athletic shoes, a water bottle, and a towel. If you're coming for a specific class like yoga, our staff can advise on any additional items you might need.

Do you offer trial memberships?

Yes! We offer a free 3-day trial so you can experience our facilities, classes, and community before committing to a membership. Simply sign up online or visit our front desk to get started with your trial.

How often should I work out to see results?

The frequency of workouts depends on your specific goals, but generally, we recommend 3-5 sessions per week for optimal results. Our trainers can help create a personalized schedule that aligns with your goals and lifestyle.
